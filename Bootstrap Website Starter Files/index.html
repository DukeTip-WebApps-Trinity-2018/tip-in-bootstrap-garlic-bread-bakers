Residential Life at Duke TIP

This is Prassel

The Residential Life at Duke Tip is awesome. Students get fed three times a day (unless breakfast is not chosen). The food is really good. Next, we do nightly activites, the activities change every night. We also get alot of free time. During free time, you can play out in the greenspace, play in the game room, or go in your room. Evey night, we have an RC (Residence Counselor) meeting every night, in the meeting, the RC will share important information and you can sign up for tomorrow's activities. Everyone should then go to bed at 10:45.
